Lazy Sunday

I have done absolutely nothing today and I’m not mad about it. Laying down and watching Netflix all day is my favorite Sunday activity. I am currently watching a series called Twin Peaks (1990). The series has two seasons (8 episodes in season 1 and 22 episodes in season 2), a total of thirty episodes. I’m on episode seventeen of the second season. ): I’m kind of sad because I’ve gotten pretty attached to this show. Although, I did read something about there being a remake or continuation of the show. I’ll have to look more into that.

Now it would be wrong to not eat ice cream on this super hot lazy Sunday. Fun fact: I follow a vegan (plant-based) diet. Dairy free ice cream for the win! :D
